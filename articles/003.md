# Three Maltese Startups Raise €12M in Q1 2026
*March 24, 2026*

Three Maltese startups have secured a combined €12 million in funding during the first quarter of 2026, marking the island's strongest fundraising quarter in three years.

## The Winners

**FinTech:** €5M for PayMed — a cross-border payment platform targeting Mediterranean healthcare

**HealthTech:** €4M for GozoHealth — telemedicine infrastructure for underserved EU islands

**Tourism:** €3M for EcoStays — sustainable tourism booking platform focused on farmhouses and heritage properties

## Why Investors Are Looking at Malta

"Malta's unique position as an EU member with strong ties to both Europe and the Middle East makes it an ideal launchpad," said Maria Vella, partner at a local VC firm.

The startups plan to hire 50+ new employees over 18 months, mostly in technical roles.

**What's next:** Q2 looks strong too — two more Maltese startups are reportedly in late-stage funding talks.
