# New Telemedicine Platform Launches for Gozo
*March 24, 2026*

Gozo residents now have access to a new telemedicine platform connecting them with specialists in Malta — no ferry trip required.

## How It Works
Video consultations through Gozo health centres, specialists available across 15 medical disciplines.

## Impact
Gozo's 30,000 residents previously needed to travel to Malta for specialist consultations. The platform cuts wait times by 40%.

"My mother used to take a whole day off for a 15-minute consultation. Now she does it from her living room," says one Gozo resident.
