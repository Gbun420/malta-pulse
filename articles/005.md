# Best Hidden Beaches in Malta This Spring
*March 24, 2026*

Spring is the best time to explore Malta's coastline — warm enough to swim, cool enough to hike, and empty enough to find your own spot.

## 1. Għajn Tuffieħa Bay (Rabat)
Less crowded than Golden Bay next door. Red-gold sand, crystal water. The 200-step descent keeps casual tourists away.

## 2. Blue Lagoon, Comino (Early Morning)
Everyone knows it, but arrive before 8am and you'll have paradise to yourself.

## 3. St. Peter's Pool
Natural swimming pool carved into limestone near Marsaxlokk. Locals know it, tourists mostly don't.

## 4. Għar Lapsi
Between Dingli Cliffs and Blue Grotto. Crystal clear water, barely marked on maps.

**Pro tip:** Visit on weekdays. Weekend crowds ruin even hidden spots.
