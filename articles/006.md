# EU Digital Services Act: How Malta Is Adapting
*March 24, 2026*

As the EU Digital Services Act takes full effect, Malta's small but tech-forward government is positioning itself as a compliance-friendly jurisdiction.

## What the DSA Requires
Platforms must provide transparency on advertising, content moderation, and algorithmic recommendations. Malta's iGaming platforms face significant operational changes.

## Malta's Approach
The Malta Digital Authority has published small-platform-specific guidelines, making compliance less burdensome than in larger member states.

"We're turning regulation into competitive advantage," says one official. "Compliance isn't a cost — it's a feature."

## Timeline
Full compliance required by Q4 2026. Most Maltese companies are on track, with 60% already compliant.
